Chicken Fried Rice



Fried rice is a staple in most Asian and Japanese restaurants. This homemade rice is even better than restaurants. The rice already has chicken in it with vegetables and it’s the perfect dish when you are craving rice.

Either chicken breast or chicken thighs may be used for easy homemade fried rice recipe. The secret to the best-fried rice recipe is basmati rice and sesame oil.

Basmati rice is a thin long grain white rice. It is typically grown in India. However, the taste is just incomparable.


So quick and easy

This is a great recipe to prep in the morning or the night before if you like to prep food and will have a busy day. Cook the rice, saute the chicken and vegetables. Then, when you’re ready to make dinner, mix everything and fry.






Ingredients

  • For The Rice:

    • 1 ½ cup basmati rice, uncooked
    • 3 cups water
    • 1 lb chicken
    • 2 carrots, grated
    • 2 eggs
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 tsp oil
    • 2 Tbsp butter
    • green onion, to taste
    • salt and pepper, to taste

  • For The Sauce:

    • 2 tsp sesame oil
    • 1/3 cup soy sauce
    • 3 Tbsp water

Instructions
  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. 🚰

  2. For best results when making fried rice, use rice that’s a day old. However, when pinched for time, I’ve let my rice cool completely and it worked just fine. 🍚

  3. f you’ll be making the rice the day before, you can also cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and marinate them. Lightly season the chicken with salt and pepper, add 1 tsp mayo + 1/2 tsp olive oil. Combine, cover and refrigerate. 🍗

  4. Either grate carrots or use a julienne slicer. Chop the onion finely. Mince garlic. 🥕

  5. Combine ingredients for the sauce. 🍽 🍜

  6. Saute chicken, vegetables and fry the egg. Add the sauce, cooked rice and dinner is ready 🥚







The tasty Chicken Fried Rice is now ready to serve
